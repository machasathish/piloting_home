Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about US flight training for Indian students.

Training in the US offers several advantages:

  • Advanced training facilities and equipment
  • International aviation exposure
  • FAA certification recognized worldwide
  • Better weather conditions for year-round training
  • Access to modern aircraft fleet

The cost typically ranges from $50,000 to $80,000 including:

  • Flight training hours
  • Ground school
  • Training materials
  • Examination fees
  • Living expenses for duration of training

Requirements for US flight training include:

  • M-1 or F-1 student visa
  • Valid passport
  • TSA approval
  • Medical certificate
  • English language proficiency

Training duration varies based on several factors:

  • Commercial pilot license: 6-8 months
  • Private pilot license: 2-3 months
  • Instrument rating: 2-3 months
  • Multi-engine rating: 2-4 weeks

Note: Duration may vary based on weather conditions, student progress, and training intensity.
